The munkaportal.ro was designed as a lightweight landing page for the jobp.eu portal. It is based on web technologies, like html, css and the good old javascript.

Even so, quite a few features was built in, like the in house translation plugin, which delivers the content in 4 languages.

Next to this, and our responsive slider, the brightest feature of the site is the job posting form. No plugins or frameworks are used for form validation, everything is handled by pure javascript. The form gets submitted to google sheets, which sends automatic notification to our admins.

All this done in pure javascript. Simple and fast.
